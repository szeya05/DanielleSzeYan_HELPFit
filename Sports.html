SPORTS


Feeling a little bit more sporty? Try out our yoga and spin classes! Something different and fun to try at aside from your usual fitness routine.



Spin class

Get ready to burn calories and boost your fitness level in this high intensity class, which is sometimes referred to as 'spinning' or 'spin'. Together with an up-beat music and our motivational trainer, push yourself as you cycle your way through multiple terrains designed to improve cardio endurance, lose weight and strengthen your legs and core.

Yoga

Want to relax but still stay in shape? Have a go at our yoga class! Be in one with your mind, body and soul while working on your flexibility and stability. Yoga strengthens, while simultaneously stretching the muscles and focusing the mind, thereby improving mind / body connection.
