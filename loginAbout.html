ABOUT US


At HELPFit gym, we believe everyone should have the opportunity to enjoy a fit and healthy lifestyle. So we have made it simple, affordable and convenient for everyone to achieve their personal health goals. Whether you are a beginner or a pro, young or old, we´re here for everybody.


WHY JOIN US?


Support


Our friendly, professional staff is trained to help you along your fitness journey, no matter how much support you need.


Tools & Training


Getting to a healthier place is about more than going to the gym. That´s why we offer personal and group training here as well as the right tools to keep you on track when you are not.


Convenience


As our gym is located near to the Sungai-Buloh MRT line and feeder busses are available within the vicinity, it makes our gym accessible to the public. Hence, you're joining the most convenient gym on the planet.


Community


You´re not just joining a regular gym. You´re joining a supportive community of like-minded people who are here to give you the encouragement you need to get through your fitness journey.
